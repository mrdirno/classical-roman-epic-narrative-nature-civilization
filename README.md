## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a chapter from the perspective of the King Stag, observing the changes Marcus brings to the mountain.
- Create a subplot about a rival family who uses Marcus's conflict with the forest to try and claim his land.
- Draft a scene where Decimus, years later, tells his children the myth of his brother and the beech.
- Write an 'alternate ending' chapter where Marcus ignores the omen and completes his ship, detailing the consequences.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive map of the Villa Valeria and the Mons Sacer, tracking the ecological changes described in the novel.
- Create a 'Soundscape of the Silva Antiqua' audio project using the descriptions of forest sounds from the text.
